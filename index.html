Description

Fairy Tail is an action-RPG that will tie in with several popular story arcs from the manga/anime series, including the Tenrou Island, Grand Magic Games and Tartaros arcs.

The developers worked with author Hiro Mashima in order to ensure that the game felt true to Fairy Tail.

Natsu Dragneel

CV: Tetsuya Kakihara

A Dragon Slayer who uses Fire Dragon Slayer Magic to embody the power of a dragon. Partners with Happy the cat and often works together with Lucy as he continues his search for his mission foster father, Igneel the Fire Dragon.

Lucy Heartfilia

CV: Aya Hirano

A Celestial Spririt Mage who has the ability to use Gate Keys to summon Clestial Spirits. She cares deeply for the Celestial Spirits, and has obtained most of the keys to the gates of Ecliptic Zodiacs. She decides to join Fairy Tail after metting Natsu and Happy, and is now an essential member of the guild.

Erza Scarlet

CV: Sayaka Ohara

Nicknamed Titania and dressed in armor, Erza Scarlet is Fairy Tail's most powerful female Mage. Using The Knight magic during battle, she can instantly swap weapons and armor with those stored in a separate space.

Gray Fullbuster

CV: Yuichi Nakamura

A Molding Mage with the ability to freely shape ice into various weapons. His abilities in battle place him among the top members of Fairy Tail, despite his odd habit of wanting to take off his clothes. He often fights with Natsu, but they maintain a friendly rivaly.

Summer Dragneel

Natsu is a word in Japanese that means summer. It's a great reference to Natsu's fiery personality and power.

Freyr

Happy's name was originally supposed to be Freyr. Hiro felt that it didn't quite feel in tune with the character's personality so it was eventually changed.

Cardcaptor Lucy

Lucy was originally intended to use cards instead of keys for her celestial spirits. It was changed because it resembled a certain anime a bit too much.

Titania, Queen of the Faeries

Erza Scarlet's title is actually a reference to a popular Shakespeare play titled "A Midsummer Night's Dream." The faerie queen in the play is as you've guessed, Titania.
